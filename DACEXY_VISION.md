# DACEXY AI PRODUCT VISION

Dacexy AI is an AI Co-Founder for small business owners.

Goal:
Help small business owners run their business with fewer employees by providing a 24/7 AI workforce.

Core Components:

1. AI Operator

* Controls Windows applications
* Controls browsers
* Automates workflows
* Performs repetitive work

2. AI Business Advisor

* Chat-based advisor
* Business strategy
* Marketing advice
* Sales advice
* Operations advice
* Finance advice

3. AI Automation Engine

* Email automation
* Reporting automation
* Research automation
* CRM automation
* Recruitment automation
* Executive assistant automation

4. AI Memory

* Remembers company information
* Remembers projects
* Remembers customers
* Remembers workflows
* Long-term memory

5. Content Studio

* Image generation
* Video generation

Target User:
Small business owners.

Priority Problems:

* Email overload
* Research
* Reporting
* Data entry
* Workflow automation
* Customer support
* Executive assistant tasks

Important:
Do not build hundreds of hardcoded features.

Build powerful reusable engines that can solve thousands of business tasks.

The final system should behave like an AI employee, AI assistant, and AI co-founder combined.
